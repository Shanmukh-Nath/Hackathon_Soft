Registration Technology

This is the frontface of the Product, from here you can navigate to core parts of the product.

This product is mainly designed to handle any specific type of event, from registration to participant handling.

Let's Understand the Hierarchy and Features of the users

  1. Superuser
    • Add Coordinator
    • Edit Participant Data
    • Edit Coordinator Sessions
    • Manage and Delete Coordinator Accounts
  2. Coordinator
    • Works Under Superuser
    • Limited Edit Access to Participant Data
    • Participant Checkin and QR Tag Assignment
    • Participant Tracker
  3. Participant
    • Can view their Details from the QR Assigned.

The Workflow of this product is as follows.

  • Participant registers through Registration Page.
  • Participant Receives a delegate pass with Event ID and Registration ID
  • On the Event Day, the Participant can approach any Coordinator and confirm their checkin by providing the OTP received on their registered email.
  • When the Checkin is Confirmed, the Participant is Assigned with a Participant ID, which is used to Identify a particular person.
  • Next Participant can Acquire a QR Tag which can be assigned to one by any Coordinator, upon scanning the QR the user is redirect to details of the Person, ofcourse details are limited and no vital details are exposed.
